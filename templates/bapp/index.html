WELCOME TO KCL CONSTRUCTION LIMITED


KCL which started off as small irrigation project in 1981 has today has defied all odds to be recognized as a spearheading force of engineering construction in India. It started its operations in the form of partnership firm in the year 1981; in the year 1999 it got incorporated as a closely held public limited company.

In these 3 decades of journey Company has emerged from a small irrigation project executing Company & has gained position in the top construction Companies of the Country. KCL approved in the category of the highest class of contractor by the relevant authorities like NHAI, State Highway & Road Development Authority, Water Supply and Sewerage Board across the Country. This truly reflects a strong penchant for quality maintained by the company.

In all these years and for the promising future to come, KCL has constantly endeavored to put the best of managerial and execution capabilities along with equipping ourselves with state-of-the-art information technology systems and equipments for effective planning, budgeting, estimating, execution and monitoring of all projects.

With a reputation of being a company that settles for nothing but the best, an unmatched track record of completing most jobs ahead of time and a penchant for going that extra mile, KCL has carved a niche for itself as one of the major players in the industry.
